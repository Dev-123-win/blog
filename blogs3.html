NOKIA 5G Smartphone New: Nokia’s phone with a 200MP camera and 6000mAh battery.

Nokia is gearing up to launch a 5G smartphone in India. Reports suggest that this smartphone may be unveiled on the auspicious occasion of the New Year. It is expected to feature an impressive design, a camera superior to a DSLR, and a long-lasting battery capable of running for up to 2 days on a single charge. Additionally, its powerful processor will support smooth gaming. Let’s explore the expected price and features of this smartphone in India.

Display

The Nokia N96 5G is expected to feature a 5.6-inch punch-hole display with a refresh rate of 90Hz and a resolution of 720×1920 pixels. It will also include a fingerprint sensor and be powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 5000 processor.

Battery

The smartphone will likely come with a massive 6100mAh battery, paired with a 25W fast charger that can fully charge the device in a short time. This battery will easily support a full day’s usage.

RAM, ROM

The smartphone is expected to be launched in three variants: [6GB RAM with 64GB internal storage], [8GB RAM with 128GB internal storage], [8GB RAM with 256GB internal storage].

Camera

In terms of the camera, the Nokia N96 5G is expected to have a 200MP primary camera, a 12MP ultra-wide lens, and a 5MP telephoto camera. For selfies, it will feature a 16MP front camera. The device will support HD video recording and offer up to 20X zoom.

expected launch & price

The Nokia N96 5G could be priced between ₹5,099 and ₹3,999. During promotional offers, discounts may reduce the price to between ₹2,999 and ₹1,499. EMI options starting from ₹999 may also be available.
