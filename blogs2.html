Vivo Best Smartphone: Vivo’s 300MP Camera with a 160W Charger Phone.

Vivo is likely to launch a new smartphone in India soon, and people are already showing great interest in it. The smartphone comes with a long-lasting battery, a DSLR-like camera, and a powerful processor. Let’s find out when this phone will be launched, its expected price, and the features it might offer.

Display

The Vivo V30 Pro 5G smartphone will feature a 6.2-inch punch-hole display with a 144Hz refresh rate. It will have a resolution of 1020×2400 pixels, a fingerprint sensor, and will be powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 7200 processor.

Battery

The Vivo V30 Pro will come with a long-lasting 6500mAh battery, and to charge it, a 65-watt charger will be provided. This charger can charge the phone quickly, and you can easily use the phone throughout the day.

RAM, ROM

Vivo is expected to launch the phone in three different variants: 8GB RAM with 256GB internal storage, 12GB RAM with 256GB internal storage, and 12GB RAM with 512GB internal storage

Camera

The rear camera will feature a 220MP primary sensor, along with a 16MP ultra-wide sensor, a 2MP depth sensor, and the front camera will be 50MP. With this phone, you can easily record 4K videos.

expected launch & price

The Vivo V30 Pro 5G is expected to be launched at a price range between ₹25,999 and ₹30,999. However, if you buy it during an offer, you might get a discount of ₹1,000 to ₹3,000, bringing the price down to ₹26,999 to ₹28,999. Additionally, you may be able to buy it on EMI starting at ₹13,999 per month. Please note that the official price and features of the phone have not been confirmed yet. The phone is expected to launch by the end of February 2025 or by the end of March 2025, but no official announcement has been made yet
