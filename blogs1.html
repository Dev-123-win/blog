BSNL 5G Phone : BSNL 200MP Camera With 6000mAh battery Phone Low Budget

BSNL is soon going to launch 5G phones in India with an impressive design. If you purchase the smartphone on EMI, you will only need to pay ₹1000. The phone is expected to come with a DSLR-like camera and a long-lasting battery, along with several other features.

Display

It is claimed that BSNL’s smartphone will feature a 6.5-inch punch hole display with a 120Hz refresh rate and a 720×1920 pixel resolution. The phone will also have a fingerprint sensor, and it will be powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 7200 processor.

Battery

The smartphone is said to come with a long-lasting 6700mAh battery. It will have a 50W charger, which will fully charge the phone in 50 to 60 minutes. Once fully charged, you will be able to use it throughout the day.

RAM, ROM, Price, and Launch

The smartphone will be available in two different variants: 6GB RAM with 128GB internal memory, and 8GB RAM with 128GB memory. The price could range from ₹5000 to ₹6000, but with special offers, it may be available for ₹2500 to ₹3000. The phone is expected to launch in March or April 2025, but an official announcement has not yet been made.

Camera

The BSNL smartphone is rumored to come with a 200MP primary camera, a 12MP ultra-wide camera, and a 13MP front camera.
